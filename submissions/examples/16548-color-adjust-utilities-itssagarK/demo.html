GSSoC Submission #16548

Color-Adjust Utilities

Control background colors, images, and border rendering styles when rendering documents to high-contrast screens or printers.

Economy Mode (Ink Saver)
.ease-color-adjust-economy
Linear Gradient

The browser is allowed to optimize or strip colors and background gradients on printers to conserve ink/toner. Gradient backgrounds might turn white or transparent.

Exact Mode (High Fidelity)
.ease-color-adjust-exact
Complex Radial Graphic

Forces the browser to print and render colors, backgrounds, and images exactly as defined in the style. Essential for charts, maps, dashboards, and visual portfolios.
